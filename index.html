About Embrace & Empower

We provide culturally sensitive, identity-affirming mentorship to individuals impacted by breast cancer, especially those from underrepresented communities.

Our mission is to ensure no one navigates breast cancer alone, with special focus on BIPOC, LGBTQIA+, men, and rural communities who face unique challenges in their cancer journey.
